About DockitScience

Welcome to DockitScience, the place you didn't know you needed. Meticulously designed to present data in a satisfying way. 1. Upload document/notes 2. Document is scanned to text 3. Text is searched for keywords. Using Google API, you can do online searches for more in depth information. Read it, save it, download it. Our mission is to redefine how data is processed and stored. We believe that information is invaluable and a lot of it is free to use. Can be used for printed documents, class notes, receipts, etc.
